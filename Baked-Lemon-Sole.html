Baked Lemon Sole

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb sole fillet
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice, fresh
  • 3 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 3 tbsp Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Season sole with salt. .
  2. Whisk oil and lemon juice. Pour into a 13x9 glass baking dish. Add fish and coat each fillet with lemon-oil mixture.
  3. Sprinkle fillets evenly with breadcrumbs, then with the cheese and paprika.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
