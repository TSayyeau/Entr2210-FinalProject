Hamburger Helper

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tbsp ketchup (or lard)
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp hot sauce (your favourite kind)
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp seasoned salt
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 cups pasta, macaroni, uncooked
  • 3 cups broth (or water)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet until no longer pink. Drain grease and return meat to pan.
  2. Reduce heat to low and add ketchup, mustard, hot sauce, seasonings, raw macaroni, and broth. Stir well to combine.
  3. Cover and simmer 12-15 minutes or until macaroni is cooked through.
  4. Remove pan from heat and stir in sour cream.
  5. Return pan to warm burner and stir in cheese. Mix until well combined.
