Beef Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup refried beans
  • 1 can green chiles, drained
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1- 1/3 cup shredded cheese, TexMex
  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • Paprika, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475F. In a large skillet, cook beef until no longer pink, breaking into crumbles;drain. Stir in beans, chiles, oregano, cumin, and salt. Cook Over medium low until heated through.
  2. Add in salsa, stir until combined.
  3. Brush one side of each tortilla with butter. Spoon 1/2 cup of mixture over half of unbuttered side.
  4. Sprinkle with 1/3 cup cheese; fold in half.
  5. Place on lightly greased baking sheet; sprinkle with paprika.
  6. Bake until crisp and golden brown, 8-10 minutes.
  7. Serve with any of your favourite toppings - sour cream, guacamole, salsa, hot sauce, etc.
