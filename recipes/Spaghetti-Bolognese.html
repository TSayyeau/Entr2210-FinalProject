Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spaghetti

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, red wine, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Serve sauce over spaghetti and enjoy!
