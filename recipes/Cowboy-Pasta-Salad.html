Cowboy Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 3 lb pasta, mini farfalle or shells
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tbsp banana peppers, pickled
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin, ground
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 tsp onion powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 4-6 tbsp canned jalapeno peppers, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, small dice
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 1 red onion, small dice
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 lb bacon, thick center cut, cooked and chopped

Dressing:

  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup sour cream (or plain Greek yogurt)
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp EACH paprika, salt, pepper

Instructions:

  1. Whisk all the dressing ingredients together. Set aside.
  2. Cook pasta until al dente. Rinse with cold water and drain. Add to large bowl and toss with HALF the dressing.
  3. Brown meat in a skillet. Drain off excess grease. Stir in chiles and all the spices. Transfer to a plate to cool.
  4. Once meat is cooled, add all ingredients to bowl with the remaining dressing and mix well.
  5. Add hot sauce for spicier, apple cider vinegar for tangier, brown sugar for less tangy if desired.
