Sweet & Spicy Tofu

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp vinegar, white
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 lb tofu, extra firm, cubed
  • 1 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced
  • Sesame seeds, for ganish

Instructions:

  1. Pan fry tofu until crisp; set aside.
  2. Mix all sauce ingredients together; set aside.
  3. Pan fry onion and peppers. Add sauce and simmer 2 minutes.
  4. Add in tofu. Cook until sauce thickens.
  5. Serve garnished with sesame seeds.
