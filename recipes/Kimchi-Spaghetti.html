Kimchi Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • 500 g pasta, spaghetti
  • 160 g kimchi, chopped
  • 80 g onion, small dice
  • 400 g bacon, chopped
  • 120 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 40 g butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
  • 1/2 cup green onion, sliced thin

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Set aside.
  2. Heat pan with butter and saute the mushrooms. Once cooked, remove and put them on a plate.
  3. Add in the bacon and stir until cooked. Add in the onion and cook until both are crispy. Remove from pan onto separate plate.
  4. Add in kimchi and stir until cooked. Once cooked, add in the mushrooms and mix together for 30 seconds. Reduce heat to medium low.
  5. Add spaghetti to pan and mix with the bacon, soy sauce, and gochujang. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Cook until heated through.
  6. Plate and garnish with the cheese, sesame seeds, and green onion.
