Donairs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp flour
  • 1 tsp oregano, ground
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 4 pitas
  • 3 tbsp flour, all-purpose
  • 1-2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, diced

Sweet Sauce:

  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar, white
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 7 tbsp vinegar (or as needed)

Instructions:

  1. For sauce: mix first 3 ingredients together. SLOWLY add vinegar, 1 tbsp at a time, until desired consistency, whisking constantly. Set aside.
  2. For the meat: mix the beef and spices well. Pick up meat and THROW it down into a steel mixing bowl 20-30 times, kneading after each time.
  3. Once done, place meat into 1 lb loaf pans and bake at 350F for 1 1/4 hours.
  4. Remove from oven and let cool. Then slice into thin slices.
  5. To Prepare: fry meat until lightly crispy.
  6. Wet pita and fry in lightly oiled pan until soft.
  7. Top pita with meat, sauce, onions, and tomatoes.
