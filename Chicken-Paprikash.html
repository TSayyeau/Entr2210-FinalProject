Chicken Paprikash

Ingredients:

  • 3 lb chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 2 tbsp butter (or lard)
  • 2 onions, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 roma tomatoes, seeded and finely diced
  • 1 Hungarian bell pepper (or banana pepper)
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3-4 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3 tbsp flour, all-purpose
  • 3/4 cup sour cream, full fat, room temperature
  • 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter (or lard) in a heavy pot and brown chicken on both sides. Transfer chicken to a plate.
  2. Add in onions and fry until golden. Add garlic, tomatoes, and peppers and fry another 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Return chicken to pot and place back on heat. Pour in broth (chicken should be mostly covered). Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 40 minutes. Remove chicken and transfer to a plate - save the sauce.
  4. In a bowl, stir the flour, sour cream, and heavy whipping cream together into a smooth paste. Stir mixture into the sauce, whisking to remove lumps.
  5. Simmer for a couple minutes until sauce thickens. Add salt and pepper to taste. Return chicken to sauce and simmer to heat through.
