Sloppy Joe Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 1 pkg. Stove Top Stuffing Mix for Chicken
  • 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 bell pepper, red, chopped
  • 1 can stewed tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup Catalina dressing
  • 1 tbsp chili powder

Instructions:

  1. Cook stuffing according to package instructions.
  2. Cook meat and drain. Add back into skillet with the peppers, ketchup, tomatoes, Catalina dressing, and chili powder. Bring to a boil; cover. Simmer 5 minutes or until pepers are tender crisp.
  3. Top with stuffing. Cover; simmer 3 minutes or until stuffing is heated through.
